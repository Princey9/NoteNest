Offline-first clinical intelligence

Turn raw clinical notes into privacy-safe, shareable summaries in seconds.

NoteNest turns messy clinical notes into safe, shareable, and useful summaries—offline by default. It redacts personal identifiers, then produces two outputs: a Patient View (plain-language explanation + next-step questions) and a Clinician View (structured SOAP-style summary with key problems, meds, findings, and plan). Designed for real workflows—referrals, second opinions, handoffs, and study—NoteNest helps patients understand their care and helps clinicians act faster, while keeping sensitive data protected.

Why NoteNest

Designed for offline workflows, fast triage, and safe sharing between clinicians, patients, and care teams.

Privacy-first redaction

Deterministic PII removal removes names, phones, emails, and address cues before summaries are generated.

Patient-friendly language

Generate a clear, gentle explanation plus questions patients can bring to their next visit.

Clinician-ready structure

SOAP-style structured output surfaces problems, meds, findings, and plans in a rapid scan.

How it works

Three quick steps for compliant summaries that stay on your machine.

1. Import a note

Paste a note, upload a file, or stream from stdin in the CLI.

2. Redact + structure

PII patterns are removed before deterministic heuristics organize the note.

3. Share outputs

Deliver patient-friendly summaries and clinician views as txt, md, or json.
